1. Plot of a Story:
Definition:

The plot is the sequence of interconnected events that make up a story.
It includes the inciting incident, rising action, climax, falling action, and resolution.

STEP 1

Exposition: Introduces the setting, characters, and initial situation. Rising Action: Builds tension and conflict. Climax: The turning point of the story. Falling Action: Ties up loose ends after the climax. Resolution: The outcome of the story.

STEP 2

A plot of land is a specific area of ground, often used for building or agriculture

STEP 3

"Plot" can also mean a secret plan, often involving some kind of scheme or conspiracy
